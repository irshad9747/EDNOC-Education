Universities in the Hungary

Stipendium Hungaricum Scholarship Program

The Stipendium Hungaricum is Hungary's flagship scholarship initiative, launched by the Hungarian Government to attract top international talent. You can apply for Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD degrees at more than 30 universities in a wide range of fields.

Applications are centralized through the stipendiumhungaricum.hu portal.

  • **Full tuition fee waiver**
  • **Monthly stipend**
    • UG: HUF 43,700
    • Master's: HUF 43,700
    • PhD: HUF 140,000–180,000
  • Free accommodation or housing allowance
  • Health insurance
  • Visa support
  • Eötvös Loránd University
  • University of Debrecen
  • Budapest University of Technology and Economics
  • University of Szeged
  • Corvinus University of Budapest
  • Engineering & IT
  • Medicine & Health Sciences
  • Business & Economics
  • Agriculture

  • Arts & Music
